Vendorspot operates as a marketplace connecting customers with independent vendors. Vendors are responsible for the accuracy, quality, and condition of the products listed and supplied through the platform.
Refunds shall only be issued in accordance with the terms stated below.

2. General Return Window
Customers may request a return of eligible items within three (3) days from the date of delivery, provided that:
The item is unused (unless defective),
The item is in its original condition,
All original packaging, tags, labels, manuals, and accessories are intact,
Proof of purchase is provided.
Returns requested outside this timeframe will not be processed.

3. Wrong Item Delivered (Vendor Liability)
Where a customer receives an item that is materially different from what was ordered (including incorrect model, specification, color, or entirely different product):
The vendor shall bear full responsibility.
The vendor shall cover all return logistics and redelivery costs.
Upon verification and confirmation of return to the vendor, the customer shall receive a full refund of:
The product price only
No administrative or processing fee shall be deducted in such circumstances.

4. Eligible Grounds for Return
A return request may be approved under the following circumstances:
	1	The item delivered is incorrect.
	2	The item is defective, damaged, or not functioning upon delivery.
	3	The item was delivered in a broken condition.
	4	The item was expired (where applicable).
	5	The item delivered was used when sold as new.
All claims are subject to validation by Vendorspot and/or the vendor.

5. Non-Returnable Items
The following categories are generally not eligible for return, except where the item delivered is wrong, defective, damaged, or inauthentic:
Underwear, lingerie, socks
Nightwear
Beachwear and swimwear
Facial skincare products
Fragrances and perfumes
Hair care products
Food items and consumables
Health and hygiene products
Personalized or customized products
Jewelry
Books
Software CDs or digital products
Bedsheets

Additionally:
Boxed electronics (including computers, mobile phones, and similar gadgets) are not eligible for return once opened, except in cases of verified defect or wrong delivery.
Items that were delivered in good condition but later damaged due to customer handling are not eligible for return.
Customized or specially produced items are strictly non-refundable.

6. Size & Fit Issues
Where the correct size was delivered but the item does not fit as expected:
Returns may be accepted for clothing and shoes only.
Delivery charges are non-refundable.
Return logistics costs shall be borne by the customer.

7. Fault After Use
If an item develops a fault after reasonable use within the return window:
The return may be approved subject to inspection and confirmation that the defect was not caused by misuse.
Clothing, consumables, and sealed boxed electronics are excluded.
